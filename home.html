About us

Local timber work, done properly.

We're a small, hands-on outfit that takes on tree work and firewood supply the way it should be done — properly seasoned logs, tidy site clearance, and no job left half-finished. Whether you need a few ton bags for the winter or a full tree felled and cleared, we turn up, do the work ourselves, and leave things better than we found them.
